About Us

Busch Gardens opened its doors in 1959 and has since become a popular animal-themed theme park for thrill-seekers!
They have two locations: the original park is in Tampa, Florida, while the sister location is in Williamsburg, Virginia.
This amusement park is my favorite because, among all the amusement parks in Florida, it offers the most heart-stopping rides in one park.
I also love that the park's animal theming is carried through to its roller coasters, often including views of the specific animal’s habitat along the ride’s path.
Most importantly, they have world-class coasters that top the list for some of the largest loops, longest tracks, and steepest drops.
